Blog

This is our live forum page where we encourage people to share their Cholinergic Urticaria stories, questions, and ideas. Whether you have overcome CU or are an everyday fighter, we gratefully appreciate your time to share what you have gone through with the illness. It's a great way to learn more about something that we don't fully understand yet and also to motivate others. It can be such a big help knowing that there are other people dealing with it too and you're not alone. I had this condition for just over 2 years and nothing made it easier to cope with then talking with others that had been through it.

Please do not post spam or anything unrelated to Cholinergic Urticaria. This page is specifically for people that have or are suffering from CU. Anything unrelated will not be posted to the site.
